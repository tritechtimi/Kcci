Departments & Ministers

Meet Our Leadership Team & Explore Specialized Ministries

Explore the dynamic ministries and dedicated ministers of Kingdom Citizens Church International. Each department is uniquely designed to nurture faith, develop spiritual gifts, and advance God's kingdom through specialized ministries and passionate leadership.

Pastoral Leadership

Our pastoral team provides visionary leadership, spiritual guidance, and dedicated service to shepherd the flock of God at Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L.

Pastor Sam Balogun

Founder & Senior Pastor
Pastor Sam Balogun is the Founder and Senior Pastor of Kingdom Citizens Church International, located on Abiola Way, Abeokuta, Ogun State. He is a Scripture-centered leader widely respected for his depth of biblical insight, clarity in teaching, and strong emphasis on spiritual understanding and practical Christian living.

His ministry is marked by sound doctrine, leadership development, and a commitment to raising believers who live out their faith with conviction and purpose.

He is the Founder of Seed Royal Bible School, an institution dedicated to equipping believers with thorough biblical knowledge and spiritual discipline. Through this training platform, numerous graduates have been empowered to impact their communities, churches, and professional spheres with the principles and teachings they received.

Beyond pastoral ministry, Pastor Sam Balogun plays a strategic leadership role in Christian education. He currently serves as the Chairman of the Christian School Owners Association of Nigeria, where he provides direction and advocacy for faith-based educational institutions across the state.

He is also the Founder of Covenant Arrows Model School, an institution committed not only to academic excellence but also to moral and spiritual formation. Under his leadership, the school has provided scholarships to underprivileged children, reflecting his passion for accessible and value-driven education.

Pastor Sam Balogun is married to Pastor (Mrs.) Felicia Balogun, and they are blessed with four children. Together, they exemplify godly leadership, family values, and a shared dedication to advancing the Kingdom of God through ministry and education.

Pastor (Mrs.) Felicia Balogun

Co-founder & Minister of Helps
Pastor (Mrs.) Felicia Balogun is a respected Christian leader, educator, and mentor whose life reflects service, compassion, and unwavering devotion to biblical truth. Fondly regarded as a “Mother in Israel,” she embodies spiritual nurture, wisdom, and steadfast commitment to the growth of families and the body of Christ.

She is a retired DDNS nurse from the Federal Medical Centre Abeokuta, where she served with distinction and professionalism. Her years in healthcare were marked by diligence, empathy, and a deep sense of responsibility toward the well-being of others. Her medical career not only demonstrated technical competence but also revealed her heart for compassionate service.

A passionate and Scripture-based Christian, Pastor (Mrs.) Felicia Balogun is deeply rooted in the Word of God. Her teachings and counsel are grounded in biblical principles, and she is known for her clarity, spiritual sensitivity, and motherly guidance in ministry.

She currently serves as the Director of Covenant Arrows Model School, where she provides strong administrative leadership and spiritual oversight. Under her direction, the institution continues to uphold academic excellence, godly character formation, and a nurturing environment for young learners.

Through ministry, healthcare, and education, Pastor (Mrs.) Felicia Balogun continues to impact lives—raising a generation grounded in faith, discipline, and purpose.

Pastor Olawale Somoye

Resident Pastor, Kingdom Citizens Church INT’L – Osaara Branch
Pastor Olawale Somoye serves as the Resident Pastor of the Osaara Branch of Kingdom Citizens Church International, where he provides faithful pastoral oversight and spiritual leadership to the congregation. With over 15 years of ministry experience, he is deeply committed to raising believers who are grounded in truth, passionate about the Kingdom, and effective in their Christian walk.

He carries a strong grace for discipleship, church administration, and small group leadership, with a heart for building systems that nurture spiritual growth and sustain the work of God. Under his leadership, the Osaara branch has witnessed steady growth in membership, spiritual depth, and community impact through consistent teaching and intentional outreach initiatives.

Pastor Somoye is particularly anointed in expository teaching, with a unique ability to break down God's Word and apply it practically to everyday life. He oversees the church's discipleship arm, Kingdom Foundations, through which over 300 believers have been firmly established in foundational Christian doctrines and Kingdom principles.

He is married to Deaconess Francisca Somoye, and together they share a burden for souls and a passion for seeing lives transformed in Osaara and its environs. Their 13-year marriage stands as a testimony of God's faithfulness and continues to inspire many couples within the church community and beyond.

Pastor Peter Oluwakunbi

Residential Pastor
Pastor Peter Oluwakunbi is a dynamic and zealous minister of the Gospel, widely recognized for his passion for youth development and sound biblical teaching. He serves at Kingdom Citizens Church International, where he previously held the position of Youth President and played a pivotal role in strengthening and expanding the church's youth ministry.

As a devoted lover of Christ and a committed teacher of the Word, Pastor Peter is known for delivering Scripture-based teachings with clarity, depth, and practical relevance. His ministry has significantly impacted the lives of many young people within the church and the surrounding community, guiding them toward spiritual maturity, moral integrity, and purposeful living.

He currently serves as the Residential Pastor at the headquarters of the church, providing pastoral oversight, spiritual leadership, and consistent mentorship to members. His leadership is marked by dedication, discipline, and a strong emphasis on spiritual growth and accountability.

Beyond pastoral ministry, Pastor Peter Oluwakunbi is the Founder of an academic institution rooted in the pursuit of excellence and spiritual understanding. The school is committed to combining high academic standards with strong moral and biblical foundations, preparing students to excel both intellectually and spiritually.

He is married to Mrs. Olaide Oluwakunbi, and together they continue to serve faithfully in ministry and community development.

Evangelist Daniel Onaleye

Evangelist & Choir Director
Evangelist Daniel Onaleye is a devoted minister and the first appointed—and currently serving—Evangelist of Kingdom Citizens Church International. He carries a distinct passion for evangelism and is deeply committed to the mandate of soul-winning and kingdom advancement.

Known for his zeal, discipline, and unwavering dedication to outreach, Evangelist Onaleye has consistently championed rigorous evangelistic efforts within the church and the broader community. His ministry is marked by bold proclamation of the Gospel, strategic outreach initiatives, and a sincere burden for the salvation and spiritual growth of souls.

In addition to his evangelistic office, he currently serves as the Choir Director, providing musical leadership and fostering a vibrant worship culture within the church. Under his direction, the choir continues to uphold excellence in worship, blending spiritual depth with musical precision.

Evangelist Daniel Onaleye is married to Mrs. Onaleye, and together they faithfully support the vision of the ministry through service, dedication, and commitment to the work of God.

Ministry Departments

Our specialized departments work together to build a strong, vibrant church community where every member can grow, serve, and fulfill their God-given purpose.

Ages 2-12

Children's Department

Raising Kingdom Kids for Divine Destiny
Our Children's church is dedicated to raising a godly generation of children who fear God, love humanity, and walk in their divine identity as kingdom citizens from an early age.

The Children's Department, also known as "Kingdom Kids Academy," provides age-appropriate biblical teaching, worship, and discipleship for children ages 2-12. We believe children are not just the church of tomorrow but vital members of the church today.

Age Groups

Nursery (2-4), Pre-school (5-7), Juniors (8-10), Pre-teens (11-12)

Weekly Programs

Sunday School, Children's Church, Wednesday Bible Club

Our vision is to see every child develop a personal relationship with Jesus, understand their identity in Christ, and begin to operate in their spiritual gifts.

Ages 13-18

Teenagers' Department

Equipping Teens for Purposeful Living
This unit is committed to guiding teenagers through the challenging transition to young adulthood with biblical principles, mentorship, and peer support.

The Teenagers' Department, called "Kingdom Teens," creates a safe, engaging environment where teenagers can explore faith, ask tough questions, and develop their God-given identity during these formative years.

Core Programs

Sunday Teen Service, Friday Night Live (FNL), Small Groups

Annual Events

Teen Camp, Career Discovery Day, Youth Evangelism Outreach

Each teen is paired with a caring adult mentor who provides guidance, prayer support, and accountability. We address relevant issues facing today's teens including peer pressure, identity, relationships, social media, academics, and career choices from a biblical perspective.

Ages 19-35

Youth Fellowship

Young Adults Walking in Wisdom and Purpose
A vibrant community of young adults committed to developing in God's wisdom, building authentic relationships, and impacting their spheres of influence.

The Youth Fellowship, known as "Kingdom Young Adults (KYA)," provides spiritual nourishment, fellowship, and practical support for single and married young adults navigating early adulthood, careers, relationships, and family life.

Specialized Groups

Career Professionals, Entrepreneurs, Students, Newly Marrieds

Skill Development

Leadership training, Financial literacy, Career counseling

We believe young adults are crucial to the advancement of God's kingdom in this generation. Through our "Kingdom Impact" initiative, members serve in various church ministries and community projects.

Royal Pillars

Women's Fellowship

Women of Strength, Wisdom, and Grace
Committed to the spiritual, emotional, and practical empowerment of women at every life stage through biblical teaching, fellowship, and supportive relationships.

The Royal Pillars Women's Fellowship is a vibrant community where women find spiritual nourishment, authentic friendships, and practical support for every season of life. Led by Pastor Mrs. Felicia Balogun, this ministry addresses the unique needs and challenges women face today.

Monthly Programs

First Saturday Fellowship, Weekly Prayer Meetings, Quarterly Seminars

Support Ministries

Prayer Warriors, Hospitality Team, Benevolence Committee

Through our "Sister-to-Sister" mentorship program, younger women are paired with mature women for guidance and discipleship. Our "Royal Business Women" network supports female entrepreneurs with training and resources.

Men of Issachar

Men's Fellowship

Men of Character, Courage, and Conviction
Equipping men to fulfill their God-given roles as spiritual leaders, faithful husbands, loving fathers, and influential kingdom agents in society.

The Men's Fellowship at Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L consists of two complementary groups: the Business Men Fellowship and the Men of Issachar. Together, they provide spiritual, professional, and personal development for men of all ages and backgrounds.

Business Men Fellowship

Monthly breakfast meetings, Business networking, Financial stewardship

Men of Issachar

Monthly prayer breakfast, Leadership training, Family enrichment

Our "Iron Sharpens Iron" mentorship program pairs younger men with mature Christian men for discipleship and accountability. Through our "Man Up" initiative, we train men to take spiritual responsibility in their homes, workplaces, and communities.

Kingdom Voices

Kingdom Citizens Voices

Ministering God's Presence Through Excellence in Worship
A dedicated team of worshippers who lead the congregation into God's presence through anointed music, vocals, and instruments, giving high premium to praise and worship.

The Kingdom Citizens Voices, under the leadership of Evangelist Daniel Onaleye, is more than just a choir—it's a worship ministry dedicated to ushering God's people into His presence through Spirit-led, excellent worship.

Ministry Teams

Main Choir, Youth Choir, Instrumentalists, Technical Crew

Musical Training

Vocal techniques, Harmony training, Sight reading

We regularly release original worship songs and have produced several albums that have blessed congregations beyond our local church. Our annual "Night of Worship" event draws worshippers from across the region for an unforgettable encounter with God.

First Impressions

Ushering Group

Creating a Worship-Friendly Atmosphere with Excellence
The frontline ministry team that maintains order, welcomes worshippers, assists with seating, distributes materials, and ensures a conducive atmosphere for worship.

The Ushering Group serves as the "first face" of Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L, creating welcoming, orderly, and worship-friendly environments for all services and events. We understand that effective ushering enhances the worship experience and facilitates ministry.

Service Areas

Greeting & Welcome, Seating Assistance, Offering Collection

Training Programs

Monthly training sessions, Quarterly workshops, Annual retreat

Our ushers are trained in hospitality, crowd management, basic first aid, emergency response, and conflict resolution. They serve not only as greeters but as ministers of helps who discreetly attend to needs during services.

Safety First

Royal Security Unit

Ensuring Safety and Order for Worry-Free Worship
The kingdom law-enforcement agents dedicated to maintaining security, orderly parking, and overall safety during all church activities and events.

The Royal Security Unit is a team of trained volunteers committed to providing a safe, secure environment for worship and ministry activities. We understand that people cannot worship freely if they feel unsafe, so we work diligently to prevent and respond to security concerns.

Security Zones

Parking Lot Security, Building Perimeter, Children's Area

Training & Equipment

CPR/First Aid certified, Conflict de-escalation, Emergency procedures

All security team members undergo background checks and receive regular training in safety protocols, emergency response, and professional conduct. We work closely with the ushering team and church leadership to ensure comprehensive coverage.

Soul Winners

Evangelism Team

Fulfilling the Great Commission Through Strategic Outreach
Passionate soul-winners committed to spreading the gospel through personal evangelism, neighborhood outreach, crusades, and innovative evangelistic strategies.

The Evangelism Team, under the leadership of Evangelist Daniel Onaleye, is dedicated to fulfilling Matthew 28:19-20—the Great Commission. We believe evangelism is not just an activity but the heartbeat of the church and every believer's responsibility.

Weekly Outreach

Saturday Evangelism, Sunday Follow-up, Wednesday Prayer Walk

Special Ministries

Prison Ministry, Hospital Visitation, Campus Evangelism

We track our outreach efforts and follow up diligently with new converts through our "New Believers' Care" program. Each new believer is assigned a mentor who disciples them for the first 90 days after conversion.

Prayer Warriors

Burden Bearers (Prayer Group)

Interceding for Breakthrough, Revival, and Divine Intervention
Dynamic individuals devoted to praying for the church, pastors, members, community, and nations—standing in the gap through fervent, strategic intercession.

The Burden Bearers Prayer Group is the spiritual engine room of Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L. We understand that prayer is not preparation for the work—it is the work. Through consistent, fervent intercession, we contend for God's purposes in our church, city, and nation.

Prayer Schedule

Monday-Friday (6am-7am), Wednesday (5pm-6pm), Friday All-Night

Prayer Focus Areas

Church Leadership, Membership Needs, Community Transformation

We maintain a 24/7 prayer chain for emergency prayer requests and operate a prayer hotline for those needing immediate intercession. Our prayer warriors are also available after services for personal prayer ministry.

Housekeepers

Sanctuary Keepers & Decorators

Maintaining Beauty and Order in God's House
Dedicated teams that keep the church building and environment clean, beautiful, and welcoming—creating an atmosphere conducive to worship and ministry.

The Sanctuary Keepers & Decorators understand that the physical environment of worship matters. We believe God deserves our best in every area, including the cleanliness, maintenance, and aesthetic presentation of His house.

Sanctuary Keepers

Weekly cleaning teams, Building maintenance, Grounds keeping

Decorators Team

Seasonal decorations, Special event setups, Floral arrangements

We operate on a rotating schedule with teams serving weekly, monthly, and for special events. Our "Adopt-a-Space" program allows individuals or families to take responsibility for specific areas of the church facility.

Tech Ministry

Media Unit (ICT Group)

Amplifying God's Message Through Technology and Creativity
Handles visual/audio production, publications, and internet outreach—leveraging technology to spread the gospel and enhance ministry effectiveness.

The Media Unit, also known as the ICT Group, serves as the technological arm of Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L. We harness the power of media and technology to amplify the gospel, document ministry activities, and enhance worship experiences.

Production Areas

Audio Engineering, Video Production, Livestream Broadcasting

Digital Platforms

Church Website, Mobile App, Social Media, Podcast

We provide comprehensive coverage of all services and events, producing high-quality recordings for distribution and archival purposes. Our livestream ministry reaches thousands of viewers weekly across multiple platforms.

Small Groups

Prayer Centre – Home Fellowship

The Church in Homes: Building Community Beyond Sunday
Neighborhood-based small groups that foster pastoral relationships, extend love and care, and provide midweek spiritual nourishment in homes across the community.

The Prayer Centre – Home Fellowship ministry extends the church beyond the four walls of the building into homes across our community. We believe that authentic Christian community happens best in smaller settings where people can connect, care for one another, and grow together.

Group Types

Geographic (by neighborhood), Demographic, Language groups

Care Ministries

Member visitation, Crisis support, New member integration

Home fellowships serve as the primary care structure for our congregation. Leaders are trained to identify needs, provide pastoral support, and connect members with appropriate church resources. They also serve as evangelistic outreach points in their communities.
