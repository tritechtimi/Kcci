Welcome to Kingdom Citizens Church International (KCCing)

Kingdom Citizens Church International (KCCing) is a vibrant, multicultural community of believers committed to nurturing heirs of God's kingdom through the gospel of Jesus Christ. Founded and led by Pastor Sam Balogun, our church is also known as the Altar of Love and Lighthouse Assembly - a place where God's love shines brightly.

At KCCing (Kingdom Citizens Church International), our mission is to raise kingdom citizens and nurture leaders who will transform their world through the power of the Holy Spirit. Through dynamic worship, sound biblical teaching, and compassionate outreach at our Altar of Love, we strive to impact our community and beyond with kingdom values.

Join us at Lighthouse Assembly as we worship, learn, and serve together in unity and love, building a church that reflects God's kingdom here on earth and prepares citizens for eternity. Under the guidance of Pastor Sam Balogun, KCCing continues to be a beacon of hope and transformation.
